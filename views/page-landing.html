Wander Space

An Educational problem solving VR game for kids using NASA and planetary data simulating realistic space environment to learn, play, grow and connect with space lovers.

Our Mission

Wander Space provides an educational, collaborative, problem solving VR experience using real data in order to inspire space exploration in schools, museums and even at home, enabling school children of all ages to interact with other planets and experience the environment on other planets.

Mars

Visit Mars, the cold red planet and the next stop on humanities voyage into the stars. Explore the lower gravity and local geology of what may become a second home.

Moon

Bright above us every night, the moon was humanities first step on another world. Experience how the world around you reacts in low gravity and no atmosphere.

About Us

We call ourselves "that-vr-team", we're a group of space enthusiasts looking to make an impact. By creating a seemless VR world for students of all ages we aim to inspire the next generation of Scientists and Engineers to go above and beyond.
